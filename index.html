TeaRounder for personal use:
TeaRounder-Anywhere

Perfect for taking drink orders at home, in the office, or as a backup option for places with no ordering system.

Just use the menu for common options and the custom item box for everything else, and you'll have a neat little list in your hand

There are a bunch of factors that go into that decision including: cost of living going up rapidly and sales being harder than I had given credit for.
