TeaRounder at-table food and drink ordering app codebase acquired by Thinkable Cloud Ltd

Thinkable Cloud has been marketing TeaRounder for some time now, so when TeaRounder creator Phil Ganney decided to finish self-employment, it made sense to give the TeaRounder at-table food and drink ordering app a new lease of life in the hands of the Buckinghamshire based e-commerce and marketing business.

At-table ordering app demos

Rob from Thinkable Cloud will be happy to demo TeaRounder for you, if you are interested in having a TeaRounder powered ordering system for your cafe or food establishment.

TeaRounder for personal use:
TeaRounder-Anywhere

A free, open-source, web-app.

Perfect for taking drink orders at home, in the office, or as a backup option for places with no ordering system.

Just use the menu for common options and the custom item box for everything else, and you'll have a neat little list in your hand

ShoppingLister

A spin-off from TeaRounder-Anywhere, but for making shopping lists.
