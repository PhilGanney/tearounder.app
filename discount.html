What is TeaRounder?

TeaRounder is a web based app primarily for phones, for ordering food and drink easily.

I designed TeaRounder with fast, convenient use in mind. The main selling point is that it gives your phone using customers a way to order, and reorder, in a really convenient way, where they don't have to physically queue and they don't need to wait for staff to come and take their order.

How much does it cost usually? What is included?

TeaRounder has a free tier that is self-service and can't do customer payment, plus 2 paid tiers: the Advanced Support package that is £30 per month at full price, and a Premium Package that is £60 per month at full price. The advanced support package adds support and setup help via video call or phone, and if you want, we can add all your menu choices into TeaRounder for you (just send us pictures or files with your menu, or even read the menu to us over a call). The Premium Package includes all of the advanced support package plus an eCommerce website (powered by WooCommerce) specifically for your business, that we connect to TeaRounder to allow payments.

TeaRounder is free for end customers, and we don't take any commission or payment fees (though payment processors do)
