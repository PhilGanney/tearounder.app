I realised that TeaRounder Anywhere is actually pretty handy for any kind of list. It just needs different options at the top and a few other little tweaks for each type of list.

So for fun, and because I know I'll use it with minimal amount of changes, I've copied all my code for TeaRounder anywhere into a new folder and am making edits to turn it into a shopping list webapp.

The recommended way to get going with this, is to play around with it.

You can remove individual items from your list, by adjusting amounts to zero - a box will then pop-up checking that you mean to remove the item. You can also remove all items by tapping the "Empty list" button - a confirmation box pops-up for that too.
